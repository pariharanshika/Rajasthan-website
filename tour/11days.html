"Cultural Rajasthan Tour 10 Days 9 Nights"

About This Tour

A Cultural Rajasthan Tour typically encompasses visits to iconic destinations in the Indian state of Rajasthan known for its rich heritage, vibrant traditions, and majestic forts and palaces. This tour often includes stops at cities like Jaipur, Udaipur, Jodhpur, and Jaisalmer, where travelers can explore historic landmarks, experience traditional music and dance, witness colorful festivals, and indulge in Rajasthani cuisine.
     

Highlights

  • 1. Explore the Pink City of Jaipur, with its vibrant culture and architecture
  • 2. Witness the grandeur of Amber Fort and take an elephant ride
  • 3. Visit Bikaner's Junagarh Fort and the famous camel breeding farm
  • 4. Experience the golden hues of Jaisalmer's fort and havelis
  • 5. Discover the blue city of Jodhpur and explore Mehrangarh Fort
  • 6. Relax in the hill station of Mount Abu with its scenic views
  • 7. Immerse yourself in Udaipur's romantic charm and City Palace
  • 8. Enjoy a boat ride on Lake Pichola and witness the sunset in Udaipur
     

Tour Plans

  • DAY 1. Arrival in Delhi
  • DAY 2. Jaipur Local Sightseeing
  • DAY 3. Jaipur to Bikaner Local sightseeing.
  • DAY 4. Bikaner to Jaisalmer.
  • DAY 5. Jaisalmer Local Sightseeing.
  • DAY 6. Jaisalmer to Jodhpur Local Sightseeing.
  • DAY 7. Jodhpur to Mount Abu Local Sightseeing.
  • DAY 8. Mount Abu to Udaipur transfer.
  • DAY 9. Udaipur Local Sightseeing.
  • DAY 10. Udaipur to Delhi.
