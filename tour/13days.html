Highlights

  • 1.Mandawa: Mandawa is renowned for its ornately decorated havelis.
  • 2.Pushkar: Pushkar is famous for its sacred Pushkar Lake and Brahma Temple.
  • 3.Delhi: Delhi's Red Fort is an iconic symbol of India's history.
  • 4.Agra: Agra is home to the magnificent Taj Mahal.
  • 5.Jaipur: Jaipur boasts the stunning Amer Fort.
  • 6.Bikaner: Bikaner is known for its impressive palaces and forts.
  • 7.Jaisalmer: Jaisalmer is famous for its golden sandstone architecture.
  • 8.Jodhpur: Jodhpur is the "Blue City" with its vibrant blue-painted houses.
