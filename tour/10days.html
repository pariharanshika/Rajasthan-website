"Rajasthan Desert Triangle Tour"

About This Tour

The Rajasthan Desert Triangle Tour is a popular travel itinerary that allows visitors to explore the desert landscapes and cultural heritage of Rajasthan, a state in northern India. The tour typically covers three major cities forming a triangle in the desert region of Rajasthan: Jaisalmer, Jodhpur, and Bikaner. This travel package includes dinner at Sam Sand Dunes in Jaisalmer, a camel safari, and nine breakfasts at all hotels. At Udaipur, take a boat ride on Lake Pichola. The cities of Bikaner, Jaisalmer, Jodhpur, and Udaipur will all provide the services of qualified English-speaking tour guides.
     

Highlights

  • 1. Begin your journey in the vibrant city of Delhi, the capital of India
  • 2. Explore the charming and artistic Havelis of Shekhawati
  • 3. Immerse yourself in the royal heritage of Bikaner, visiting Junagarh Fort and more
  • 4. Discover the golden city of Jaisalmer, including its fort, havelis, and the mystical Sam Sand Dunes
  • 5. Witness the beauty of the desert at Sam Sand Dunes with a camel safari
  • 6. Explore the cultural richness of Jodhpur, visiting Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, and more
  • 7. Experience the bustling streets and historical landmarks of Delhi, including the iconic Red Fort and Qutub Minar
  • 8. Conclude your journey with a heart full of memories and depart from Delhi
